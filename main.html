Maldives - A Tropical Paradise

The Maldives, a tropical paradise in the Indian Ocean, is known for its stunning coral reefs, white sandy beaches, and crystal-clear turquoise waters.

Must-Try Foods

  • Mas Huni: A traditional Maldivian breakfast dish made with tuna, coconut, onion, and chili.
  • Fihunu Mas: Grilled fish seasoned with local spices, a true Maldivian delight.
  • Rihaakuru: A thick fish paste, often used as a condiment in Maldivian cuisine.
  • Bis Keemiya: Deep-fried pastries filled with fish, coconut, and spices.
  • Kulhimas: Spicy tuna salad, perfect for seafood enthusiasts.
